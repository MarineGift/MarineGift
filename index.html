세계 최초 친환경 기저귀, 생리대 개발

여성들과 아기들은 살아가면서 일회용 생리대와 기저귀를 사용해야 합니다.

하지만 생리대와 기저귀에 사용되는 흡수체는 미세플라스틱으로 제조되어 환경을 파괴합니다.
또한 강력한 화학흡수력으로 생식기 주변의 수분을 흡수하기에 일회용 기저귀 사용시 아기에게 불임의 원인이 될수 있다는 독일 킬대학 연구진의 발표가 있었기에 더욱 불안합니다.

생리대 파동으로 인해 국내 프리미엄 생리대는 소비자들의 외면으로 제조사가 사용을 하지 않는 화학흡수체가 기저귀는 최소 5배이상 많이 사용되어 건강에 매우 좋지 않습니다.

전 세계 수많은 기업들이 천연 흡수체 개발을 위해 노력하고 있지만 현재까지 상용화 하지 못하고 있습니다. 친환경 바다소재로 여성과 아기의 건강을 지키기 위해 창업했습니다.

아라메친환경소재연구소는 바다 나노섬유 천연그물망으로 환경문제 없으며 사용자의 건강에 안전하며 화학흡수체를 100% 완벽히 대체한 친환경 흡수체를 개발하여 국내 특허등록 및 PCT 출원 하였습니다.

유기농 생리대 세계적인 브랜드 나트라케어 대비 동일한 소재, 바다흡수체 사용으로 더 뛰어난 흡수력, 합리적인 가격으로 한국을 넘어 세계 시장에 진출 하려고 합니다.

eco-Friendly Bio Products from Marine materials

Pioneer of Marine materials

World First Marine Fiber developed by Prof.Seo & his research team!

We research, develop and produce eco-friendly bio products that are essential for a healthy well-being life. We have been producing seaweed cellurose hydrogel facial mask, Bio-degradable Diaper, Sanitary Napkin using Bio Super Absorbent from Seaweed extract, Seaweed Bar Soap, HEPA filter from red algae for the first time in the world.

Biodegradable
Our natural superabsorbent polymer is biodegradable and renewable in nature. In laboratory testing we were able to achieve near 100% biodegradability in just 30 days. This biodegradable feature makes it an excellent alternative to fossil fuel-based polymers used in a wide range of consumer products including disposable diapers and sanitary pads, which are nondegradable. Disposable diapers are the third largest contributors to landfills in the world. Our natural superabsorbent polymer can solve this problem and can serve as a sustainable and environmentally friendly material in many applications. Simply put, we can help reduce the microplastic waste in the environment.

Safe
It has been reported that traditional SAP, a sodium polyacrylate power, may cause respiratory tract and lung irritation when inhaled. Moreover, SAP has linked to health problems such as Toxic Shock Syndrome, and thus in 1980’s United States has banned it from using in tampons. Unlike the traditional SAP, our products are produced from all natural materials. Hence consumers can rest assured that they are indisputably safe.

Super Absorbent
The number one performance consideration when purchasing a disposable diaper is absorption. Our superabsorbent natural polymer has superior absorbency rate and excellent permeability that meets the demanding specifications of the disposable diaper industry.

Commercially Scalable
We utilize abundantly available natural marine materials available on the earth such as seaweed, kelp, algae, and even seafood waste. This enables us to develop product that is practical and scalable for commercial use.

Cost Effetive
While there have been a number of research conducted to develop biodegradable superabsorbent polymer from a variety of natural sources, none has emerged as being commercially viable until now. We have demonstrated that we can source marine material that is more cost effective, and we have developed a manufacturing process that’s more scalable that can reduce cost.
